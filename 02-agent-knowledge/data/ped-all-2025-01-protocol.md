# PROTOCOL BRIEF (sample, fictional) — PED-ALL-2025-01

## Title
A Multi-Arm Adaptive Trial of Targeted Salvage Therapy in Relapsed Pediatric
B-cell Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia (B-ALL).

## Phase
Phase II, multi-institutional, adaptive.

## Primary Objective
To estimate the complete response rate (CR + CRi) at the end of induction
(Day 29 ± 3) in pediatric patients (ages 1–21) with first relapse of B-ALL,
stratified by *IKZF1* deletion status.

## Secondary Objectives
- Event-free survival (EFS) at 12 and 24 months.
- Frequency and severity of cytokine release syndrome (CRS), graded per
  ASTCT 2019 consensus.
- Minimal residual disease (MRD) negativity rate at Day 29 by 10⁻⁴
  flow cytometry.

## Key Inclusion Criteria
1. Age 1 through 21 years at enrollment.
2. Morphologically confirmed first relapse of B-ALL.
3. Karnofsky / Lansky performance status ≥ 50.
4. Adequate organ function (ALT/AST ≤ 5× ULN; creatinine clearance ≥ 60
   mL/min/1.73 m²).

## Key Exclusion Criteria
- Prior CD19-directed CAR-T within the preceding 90 days.
- Active, uncontrolled CNS-3 disease (defined as ≥ 5 WBC/µL with blasts in CSF).
- Pregnancy or breastfeeding.

## Treatment Arms
- **Arm A:** Standard reinduction (4-drug) + experimental targeted agent
  *EX-1042* (oral, 25 mg/m²/day, Days 1–14).
- **Arm B:** Standard reinduction (4-drug) + placebo.

Randomization is 2:1 (Arm A : Arm B) with adaptive re-randomization at the
interim analysis (after the first 40 evaluable patients).

## Stopping Rules
- **Safety:** Pause enrollment if Grade ≥ 4 CRS rate in Arm A exceeds 15%.
- **Futility:** Halt for futility if the conditional probability of meeting
  the primary endpoint at the planned final analysis falls below 10%.

## Biospecimen Collection
- Bone marrow aspirate at Day 0, Day 15, and Day 29.
- Peripheral blood for cfDNA at Days 0, 8, 15, 22, 29.
- Optional tumor biopsy at relapse for whole-exome and RNA-seq.

## Data & Safety Monitoring
Independent DSMB reviews every 6 months or after every 25 evaluable patients,
whichever comes first.
